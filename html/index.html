About

I am Kathleen Danielle Manguele and I am a student in web design at Century College. Since my childhood, I have always created designs and worked with computers. When I graduated from high school, I wanted to follow that path. Then I enrolled at the university in my home country (Cameroon) where I went for a computer science program and obtained a bachelor's degree. However, I felt I needed more of a design perspective to succeed so I went for a degree in web design and here I am. My best attributes as a web designer are creativity, originality and organization. I enjoy building original and unique websites.
